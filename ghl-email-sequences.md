## SEQUENCE 1: Welcome Series (New Subscriber)

**Trigger:** Contact added to "CHG Subscriber" tag

### Email 1: Welcome (Send immediately)
**Subject:** Welcome to Connective Health Global

Welcome to the community. Every week, we bring you the latest chronic illness research, evidence updates, and new provider listings — all graded by our published evidence rubric.

Here's what to expect:
- Weekly research alerts (new studies, evidence changes)
- Provider spotlights (new clinics and practitioners)
- Supplement evidence updates

In the meantime, here are three places to start:
1. Browse our condition directory
2. Explore the research library
3. Check out evidence-graded supplements

Welcome aboard.

### Email 2: How Evidence Works (Day 3)
**Subject:** How we rate what we publish

One question we get a lot: how do you decide what to include?

Every listing in our directory is rated using a published evidence rubric:

[CLINICAL TRIAL] - Supported by RCT or systematic review
[MULTIPLE STUDIES] - Supported by 2+ independent studies
[PATIENT REPORTS] - Open-label or patient-reported data only
[EMERGING] - Preliminary or theoretical evidence only

We never accept payment for listings. The evidence gate is structurally independent of commerce. Only CLINICAL TRIAL and MULTIPLE STUDIES rated supplements carry affiliate links — and every link cites its original source.

Trust first. Revenue follows.
